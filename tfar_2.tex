Let $f(x)$ be integrable. And suppose that

$$\int_0^1 (4x^3 - f(x))f(x) dx = \frac{4}{7}$$

Find $\int_0^1 f(x) dx$.

Solution: See the title.

$$\int_0^{1}\left(2x^3-f(x)\right)^2\, dx=0$$
